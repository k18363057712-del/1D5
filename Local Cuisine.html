Cuisine Introduction

1. Lotus Flower Crisp

Selected lard, flour, and lotus seed paste, wrapped and fried to perfection. Crispy texture and lifelike shape.

2. Leaping Baotu

A miniature landscape of Baotu Spring, with an ethereal mist.

3. Crispy Lotus Root with Ginger

Light flavor, crisp and refreshing.

4. Fulin Roast Duck

Looks similar to Peking duck, but the crispy skin is even more fragrant and crunchy.

5. Cattail with Milk Soup

Made with milk soup and cattail, the soup is milky white, enhancing the crisp and fresh flavor of the cattail. Light and delicious.

6. Furong Lake Shrimp

This dish selects fresh green shrimp from Baiyun Lake, shelled and combined with mountain chicken egg whites, stir-fried using traditional soft-cooking techniques, highlighting its elegant and lasting charm.

7. Tea-scented Abalone in a Cup

This dish uses Heihu Spring water, locally grown lotus seeds from Jinan, and Lingyan tea as the main ingredients, paired with Jiaodong wild abalone. It has a fresh taste and a golden-yellow color.
